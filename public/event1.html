Northwestern Regionals Spring 2020

February 22, 2020 • Hosted by Lambda Delta Chapter

Theta Tau brothers from every chapter attend a regional conference each semester. This semester, the conference was hosted by Lambda Delta Chapter at the University of the Pacific.

The regional conference is an opportunity for brothers to network, gain new perspectives on chapter operations, and create stronger bonds among themselves. This semester’s regional conference’s theme was ‘PHIRED UP.’ The conference focused around the ideas of effective recruitment and retention.

The conference also serves as a way for the national fraternity officers to update the chapters on matters pertaining to the fraternity's goals and current areas of focus.

The conference gave the members of our chapter time to bond and make lasting memories.
